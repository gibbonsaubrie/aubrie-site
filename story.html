Our Story

Executive Auto Glass

We are a small, locally owned and operated family business. As a long established business of Charlotte County, we pride ourselves on delivering great customer service. We strive to provide our customers with quick, professional on site service while using top quality parts and materials.

Our services include:
  • free mobile service
  • motorhome and RV glass replacement
  • window motors and regulators
  • sunroof repairs
  • windshield repairs
  • water leak repairs
  • rust repair
  • and much more!
When having a windshield replaced in your vehicle, you can be assured you are getting quality parts backed by our lifetime workmanship warranty.

Over the years we have built long standing relationships with the many body shops, dealerships, and insurance customers. We look forward to continuing to grow those relationships and our professional customer service staff look forward to helping you with any of your auto glass needs.

Our Warranty & Promise

All windshield and back window installations are guaranteed against leaking for as long as you own the vehicle and unlimited mileage unless noted otherwise. Windshield repairs are warranted for as long as you own the vehicle and unlimited mileage. If the repair should crack or run from the original point of repair, Executive Auto Glass and Repair, Inc. will apply the cost of the repair toward the replacement of a new windshield.

Door glass installation and workmanship is guaranteed for 90 days.
